Overview

Langston Hughes, a pivotal figure of the Harlem Renaissance, was an American poet, novelist and playwriter. His works would celebrate and aluminate the African American experience, as well as addressing the social issues of his time with. A lot o his writings do a good job at capturing the essense of the human spirit amidst struggle and resilience.
